# Cover Letter Review Checklist

Evaluate each section. Give specific feedback, not just "good" or "needs work."

## Opening
- Hook: specific to this company/role, or could go to anyone?
- References something concrete about the company?
- No cliches ("I am writing to express my interest...")

## Body
- Every claim backed by a specific example?
- At least 1-2 quantified results?
- Connects to JD requirements? Shows don't tells?
- Focused on 2-3 key qualifications (not everything)?

## Company Knowledge
- Shows genuine understanding, not surface-level facts?
- Tied to why the applicant wants to work there?

## Closing
- Confident but not arrogant?
- Clear next step? Forward-looking (what they'd contribute)?

## Tone & Style
- Sounds like a real person, not AI-generated?
- Consistent tone throughout? Personality comes through?
- Matches user's reference cover letter style?

## Structure
- Under 400 words? 3-4 paragraphs max?
- Complements resume (adds context, doesn't restate bullets)?

## Summary Output
1. Strongest section
2. Weakest section
3. Top 3 specific changes (ranked by impact)
4. Overall: Ready / Minor edits / Significant revision needed
