# Career Self-Assessment

Ask one question at a time. Follow up on interesting answers. Use multiple choice when possible.

## Questions

**Work type:** What energizes you — building, analyzing data, researching, designing systems, working with people? (multiple choice)

**Activities:** Which do you enjoy — writing code, exploring data, reading papers, optimizing systems, collaborating with non-technical people, teaching?

**Technologies:** What tools/languages do you gravitate toward? Why — the tools themselves or what you build with them?

**Industries:** Which interest you — tech, finance, healthcare, AI, e-commerce, social impact, education, other? (multiple choice)

**Mission:** Does company mission matter? (Very much / Somewhat / Not really)

**Environment:** Startup / growth / enterprise / research lab? (multiple choice)

**Work-life balance:** Very important / Moderate / Low priority right now?

**Remote/hybrid/office?** Team size preference — small/medium/large/independent?

**Short-term goals (1-2 years)?** Specialize deeply or explore broadly?

**5-year vision:** Senior IC / tech lead / manager / founder / researcher / unsure?

**What does success look like to you?** (open-ended)

**Rank in order:** compensation, learning, work-life balance, prestige, mission, location flexibility, team quality

**Location?** Flexible / specific area / remote only?

**Compensation expectations?** Share typical ranges if unsure.

## Synthesize

After completing: summarize profile (3-4 sentences), key themes with evidence, ranked priorities, tensions between answers, recommendations for job search focus.
