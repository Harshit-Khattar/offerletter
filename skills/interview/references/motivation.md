# Motivation & Questions to Ask

## "Why This Company/Role?" Frameworks

**"Why [Company]?"** — specific company thing → connect to your values → how you'd contribute. Never: "great company", "compensation", "need a job"

**"Why this role?"** — your matching skills → what excites you about the role specifically → career fit

**"How would you contribute?"** — one quick win from your strongest skill → longer-term growth impact

**"What attracts you to our industry?"** — genuine personal connection → specific knowledge → authentic enthusiasm

**"Where do you see yourself growing?"** — learn (6-12mo) → contribute (1-2yr) → grow toward (3-5yr). Never: "in your seat", "starting my own company"

## Questions to Ask Interviewers (10-12)

**Role/Technical:** "Typical day/week?" | "Tech stack and how you adopt new tools?" | "Most impactful project shipped recently?"

**Team/Culture:** "How does code review work?" | "What does onboarding look like?" | "Working style — independent or collaborative?"

**Growth:** "Learning/development opportunities?" | "Career development check-ins?" | "What makes someone successful in this role?"

**Strategy:** "Biggest challenges the team faces?" | "Where's the product heading next year?" | "What surprised you about working here?"

**Avoid asking:** what the company does (no research), salary (first round), promotion speed (entitled), Glassdoor complaints (defensive)
