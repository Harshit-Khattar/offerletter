# Informational Interview Reference

For networking conversations, not formal interviews. Goal: learn and build connections, not ask for jobs.

## Generate 5-6 Strategic Questions

Progress from broad → specific: career path → daily reality → industry insight → role details → career development → networking closer.

**Required categories:** daily responsibilities, career development, growth opportunities.

## Calibrate to Relationship

**Alumni:** leverage shared school experience ("How did our program prepare you?")
**LinkedIn cold:** more professional, show you did homework ("I noticed you transitioned from X to Y...")
**Conference contact:** reference their talk/topic
**Referral:** mention the connection, ask for insider perspective

## Always End With

"Is there anyone else you'd recommend I speak with?" — the most valuable outcome is often the second connection.

## Rules

- 5-6 questions max (20-30 min conversation)
- Never ask about job openings or referrals directly
- Include strategic purpose for each question
- Remind user: send thank-you within 24 hours
